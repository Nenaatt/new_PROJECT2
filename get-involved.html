VOLUNTEERING

Starting out as a small group of people passionate about helping others, Bright Future Charity quickly grew via word-of-mouth. Today, we consist of volunteers from all walks of life.

Volunteer Roles
  • Minimum age is 18 years old for street distribution.
  • For other activities, minimum age is 12 years old and accompanied by a guardian.
  • First-time volunteers are required to attend a briefing.
  • No jewelry. No shorts, tight and low-cut revealing tops. No high-heel shoes.
  • No religious preaching. No politics.
  • Transport is not provided.
  • Street distribution may last till past midnight.

DONATION

As an NGO, our operations are dependent on donations from corporations and the public. Whether monetary or in-kind, they help us continue our mission of serving the underprivileged.

Food (Dry)

Rice, biscuit, butter, canned baked beans, canned sardines, chocolate-based drinks, condensed milk, cooking oil, dry/instant noodles, family milk powder, flour, instant oats, jam, margarine, salt, sugar, etc.

Food (Wet)

Bread, bun, cooked food (Halal), banana, etc.

Non-Food items

Disposable face masks, disposable gloves, adult diapers, first-aid items, hair shampoo, medicated oil, mosquito coils, soap, etc.

PARTNERSHIPS

We can work together to benefit the underprivileged communities of our nation through practical charity initiatives. This additionally offers employees and customers a valuable opportunity to participate in volunteering experiences, both directly and indirectly.

We are firm believers in non-discrimination and practice this by helping where there is a need, regardless of race and religion. Our goal is to reach more people in the marginalized communities we serve; as such, we welcome assistance from the public.

If interested, contact us at BrightFutureCharity.com
